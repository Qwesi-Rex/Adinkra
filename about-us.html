ABOUT US

Our Story

Following the strategic exit of British American Tobacco from key markets, Adinkra SA has risen to champion the tobacco sector with a deep commitment to ethical practices and regional growth. Today, we are expanding our horizons to others FMCGs categories to position ourselves as a multi-category player to seize untapped opportunities in Sub-Saharan Africa Frontier Markets set for tremendous growth.

Our Purpose

The global Market has faced unprecedented changes in the recent years where volatility and rapid trends shift are setting the new rules.

African Market are not exempt from above facts and in addition to this, they have various peculiarities that make opportunities they offer difficult to seize.

As Distribution professional, we intend to bridge the gap between international companies and the local players and make the downstream Market more profitable for them while ensuring a sustainable business for all stackholders
