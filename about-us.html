ABOUT US

Our Story

Following the strategic exit of British American Tobacco from key markets, Adinkra SA has risen to champion the tobacco sector with a deep commitment to ethical practices and regional growth. Today, we are expanding our horizons to others FMCGs categories to position ourselves as a multi-category player to seize untapped opportunities in Sub-Saharan Africa Frontier Markets set for tremendous growth.

Our Purpose

The global Market has faced unprecedented changes in the recent years where volatility and rapid trends shift are setting the new rules. African Market are not exempt from above facts and in addition to this, they have various peculiarities that make opportunities they offer difficult to seize. As Distribution professional, we intend to bridge the gap between international companies and the local players and make the downstream Market more profitable for them while ensuring a sustainable business for all stackholders

Freddy Sollen-Messanvi

Managing Director, Adinkra Group

Welcome to Adinkra Group

At Adinkra Group, we are committed to becoming the leading distributor of Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) across West Africa. With a focus on delivering value, quality, and reliability, we aim to cater to the diverse needs of our customers and communities.

The name Adinkra is deeply rooted in West African heritage, symbolizing unity, strength, and shared values. Just as Adinkra symbols are used to communicate key messages and cultural wisdom, we aspire to build a business that represents excellence, trust, and innovation in every market we serve. While our roots are in the tobacco industry, we are evolving to expand our portfolio, becoming a trusted partner for a wide range of FMCG products. Our vision is to bring everyday essentials to millions of households, contributing to the sustainable growth of the region while upholding the integrity and richness of our heritage.

We believe that strong partnerships and an unwavering commitment to excellence will drive us forward as we continue to innovate, adapt, and build a brighter future for West Africa. Thank you for being a part of our journey.

Services

Adinkra SA manufactures and distributes a broad selection of tobacco products, including well-known brands like Craven A and Concorde. We are committed to meeting the evolving needs of adult consumers in West Africa, with plans to expand into nicotine-based products for a safer future.
